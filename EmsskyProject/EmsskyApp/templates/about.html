About EMS Skynet

Founded in 2018 by Emmanuel A. Festus, EMS SKYNET KREA8TIVE CONCEPT stands among Nigeria’s foremost ICT and business innovation hubs. We specialize in computer training, digital design, and business services— empowering individuals and organizations through creativity and technology.

Our mission is simple: make technology accessible and provide reliable solutions that help businesses and learners grow.

Over the years, EMS SKYNET has expanded into a full-service cyber café and business center, offering printing, photocopying, document processing, embassy form filling, and fast, secure internet access. Our vision is to make every aspect of technology and business services seamless and approachable for all clients. We believe that innovation thrives in an environment where knowledge, creativity, and resources are easily available. EMS SKYNET’s journey began with a single computer and an unwavering vision: to make technology simple, accessible, and meaningful for everyone. From humble beginnings, we recognized that true progress comes from bridging knowledge gaps and equipping individuals and businesses with the tools they need to thrive in a digital world. Our classrooms are designed to foster hands-on learning, our design studio crafts impactful digital identities, and our business center ensures that every administrative and technical need is handled efficiently. At EMS SKYNET, we believe that technology should empower, not intimidate. Each project, training session, or service we provide is guided by the principle of excellence and a commitment to our clients’ growth. Our mission remains clear: to inspire, educate, and equip people for a future where technology and creativity go hand-in-hand.
